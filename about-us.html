About the Company

Our History

  • Our Humble Beginnings

    Citiad Promo & Prints Co. traces its humble beginnings from Pintaad Graphix & Designs a sole proprietorship that offers graphics, exhibition logistics and printing services in Cainta, Rizal in 2004.

  • The Growth of the Company

    The company continued to grow and started to handle significantly bigger projects which led to the addition of the event management as part to its service offerings.

  • Company Expansion

    The company expanded its business with the birth of Citiad Promo & Prints Co. a Filipino company duly registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission. It has its principal office address at Unit C3 Bueno Tierra Building, 4580 Lilac Street,SSS Village, Concepcion 2, Marikina City.

  • Who we are now

    Citiad Delivers good satisfying events, high class promotional giveaways, and high quality printing services for professional associations, corporations, small to large scale companies and government agencies.
